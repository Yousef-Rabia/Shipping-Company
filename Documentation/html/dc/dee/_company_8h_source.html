Shipping Company 0.1
A shipping company needs to handle cargo delivery the most efficient and profitable way.The company needs to automate the cargo-truck assignment process to achieve good and fair use of its trucks.Fortunately,the company manager discovers your programming skills and your deep knowledge of different data structures and decides to hire you to developaprogram that simulates the operation of the cargo delivery process and calculates some related statistics in order to help improve the overall process.
